To copy or paste large text files into your python programs, use the python pyperclip module. 

To install, EX) 
C:\Users\<USER>\Program Files\Python 3.5\Scripts>pip.exe install pyperclip 


Use the copy() and paste() pyperclip functions to copy and paste text into your programs:

>>>pyperclip.copy('Hello World")

>>>pyperclip.paste()
'Hello World'
